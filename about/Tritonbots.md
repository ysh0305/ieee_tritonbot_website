# UCSD IEEE TritonBots Team

This page will review the v2026 hardware and software changes to the TritonBots RoboCup Small Size League team at the University of California, San Diego. The team intends to compete at RoboCup 2026 in Incheon, South Korea. Changes and new developments to the electronics, mechanical design, AI systems and control systems will be discussed in detail, highlighting the improvements and progression the team has made over the course of the RoboCup 2026 season.
