# 2.2 Chassis/Drivetrain

The second version of the baseplate possesses a similar cutoff profile to last year’s version, with different hole positioning.

The support for the electronic housing and the robot coverage is now provided, not directly by attachment to the baseplate, but rather by pins on top of the motor mounts.

This reduces the deformation of the base plate, as less load and moment is applied to the baseplate and more towards the wheels.
